About WC Perway

Dedicated to building and maintaining South Africa's essential infrastructure with precision and reliability.

Our Story

WC Perway, based in Vanderbijlpark, has proudly served clients across the Western Cape and greater South Africa for over 8 years. We specialize in rail infrastructure development and maintenance, alongside a comprehensive range of civil construction services. Our expertise also extends to the installation of modern solar systems and the provision of high-quality scaffolding solutions.

Our mission is to deliver projects that not only meet but exceed client expectations. We are built on a foundation of delivering quality workmanship, adhering to stringent safety standards, and ensuring on-time project completion. We understand the critical role our work plays in the broader economy and community, and we approach every task with the utmost dedication.

Our Commitment

  • Quality: We use high-grade materials and skilled professionals to ensure durability and excellence in every project.
  • Safety: Safety is paramount. We adhere to strict safety protocols to protect our team, our clients, and the public.
  • On-Time Results: We value your time. Our efficient project management ensures timely completion without compromising on quality.
